Frequently Asked Questions: UK Sponsorship Checker Guide

What is a UK Skilled Worker sponsorship licence checker?

A UK Skilled Worker sponsorship checker is an interactive search directory that references the official Home Office register of licensed worker sponsors. This tool helps job seekers, recruiters, and compliance officers instantly verify if a specific UK employer has the legal licence required to sponsor foreign worker visas (such as the Skilled Worker route or Global Business Mobility route).

Our database synchronizes daily with the official government records, housing over 141,000+ active licensed sponsor companies in the United Kingdom.

How do I verify if a UK company can sponsor visa applications?

To verify if a UK employer can sponsor visa applications, type the company name into the search bar of our UK SponsorRadar checker dashboard. You can instantly filter the results by Town/City, Licence Rating, and specific visa routes (e.g., Skilled Worker or Temporary Worker). If the company is listed with an active status, they hold a valid Home Office sponsorship licence.

Once you locate a verified sponsor, click on their row to open the Job Research Center, which provides direct links to search their live vacancies on LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor.

What is the difference between an A-rating and a B-rating sponsorship licence?

The Home Office assigns compliance ratings to all UK sponsor licence holders:

A-Rating: This represents the highest compliance rating. It proves the UK employer is fully compliant with all sponsorship duties and has a clean record. An A-rated sponsor can issue Certificates of Sponsorship (CoS) and sponsor new skilled workers directly.

B-Rating: This rating is given if the Home Office finds compliance breaches or administrative errors. B-rated sponsors are placed on an action plan to address issues and **cannot** sponsor new workers until they upgrade back to an A-rating. Our dashboard highlights these compliance ratings clearly so you can identify safe employers.

How does the dynamic daily sync track new sponsorship companies?

Our server-assisted backend connects securely to the live GOV.UK publishing servers once every 24 hours. The scraper compares the new guidance CSV file with our existing SQLite database. It isolates newly added companies and marks them as "Newly Added" on your dashboard. Similarly, it archives companies that were suspended or removed by the Home Office. This provides job seekers with a powerful list of fresh sponsor licence arrivals who are highly likely to be actively hiring right now.
