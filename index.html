Frequently Asked Questions: UK Sponsorship Careers Guide

What is ukSponsorJobs?

ukSponsorJobs is a premium Skilled Worker visa sponsorship careers platform. We crawl and check the official UK register of licensed sponsors and index live job openings straight from their corporate career pages. This merges sponsorship validation with active job searches.

How do I verify if a UK company can sponsor visa applications?

To verify if a company can sponsor you, head over to the Sponsor Directory tab and search their name. If they appear with an active status and an A-Rating, they are licensed by the Home Office to issue Certificates of Sponsorship (CoS).

How does the automated Careers Web Spider crawl listings?

Our background crawler runs continuous logarithmic search paths to discover corporate careers URLs for registered visa sponsors. It visits their vacancy listings, extracts role requirements, compiles bullet-points and paragraphs into rich job descriptions, and renders them instantly in our sliding detail panel.
